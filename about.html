A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Fitness For Life is a global fitness community specializing in innovative, high-intensity group workouts that are fast, fun, and results-driven.

As of August 2022, Fitness For Life was the only class-based franchise company that was ranked in the top 40 fastest growing franchises in the world and was ranked #1 as the world’s fastest-growing gym franchise.

“We believe our growth can be attributed to our three key pillars: Innovation of workouts; Motivation from training in team-based classes; and Results from Fitness For Life deploying HIIT as the modality that achieves the best results for clients looking for life-changing outcomes. We believe that we can become the fastest-growing franchise in the world and accelerate past household names such as McDonalds and 7-Eleven. ”

OUR HISTORY

  • 2018 -Establishment of Fitness for Life with single branch with a challenging decision of making every individual healthy and fit.
  • 2019 -After the success of its first branch, Three new branches were established in different locations and first branch success brought up good number of new registrations.
  • 2020 -Establishment of new chain,Fitness For Life marked as new franchise that builds fit nation and overallgrowth leading to a healthy nation.
  • 2021 -Best Fitness centre Award and Global wide recognization ,followed by many kind activites and sustainable development.
  • 2022 -Our franchise ranked 47th on Entrepreneur's Franchise 100 in 2022, and 96th in 2021, as well as #6th on Entrepreneur's Fastest Growing Fitness Franchise 500 in 2020. They also were named one of Fast Company's 'Most Innovative Wellness Companies of 2020.
